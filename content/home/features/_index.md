### New Features Coming Soon

Open Ecoacoustics is part of a growing project, supported by the [Australian Research Data Commons (ARDC)](https://ardc.edu.au/). With this continued support, we continue to develop and integrate new features to accelerate environmental research. Exciting new features are being developed through a project with the ARDC, including:

- BirdNET & Perch embeddings for all recordings
- Notebooks for species recognition
- Species detections data will be shared with the [Atlas of Living Australia (ALA)](https://www.ala.org.au/) and others
- Downstream analysis notebooks will be available on [EcoCommons Australia](https://www.ecocommons.org.au/)
