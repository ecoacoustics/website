### Why It Matters

Ecoacoustics is transforming how we monitor biodiversity. By contributing your data or exploring existing recordings, you’re helping to:

- Detect rare and threatened species
- Train AI species identification tools like BirdNET and Perch
- Build scalable, open, and rigorous monitoring systems
